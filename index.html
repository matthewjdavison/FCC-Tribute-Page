Sean Connery

Sir Sean Connery was a Scottish actor most known for playing the first 'James Bond'.
  • Connery began acting in a small theatres and television productions until his breakout role as James Bond.
  • Featured in famous movies such as:
    • 'Murder on the Orient Express',
    • 'Untouchables',
    • 'Dragonheart',
    • 'Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade'.
  • His achievements in film were recognised with an Academy Award, two BAFTAs and three Golden Globes.
